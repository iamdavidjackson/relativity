Relativity

The name “theory of relativity” is connected with the fact that motion from the point of view of possible experience always appears as the relative motion of one object with respect to another ... Motion is never observable as “motion with respect to space” or, as it has been expressed, “absolute motion” Albert Einstein - "The Theory of Relativity", 1949
